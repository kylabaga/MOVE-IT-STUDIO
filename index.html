DANCE

It is an art form that is performed by dancers, as a solo or a group dance. Dancing is frequently accompanied with music and can be used for expression of thoughts or emotion, has a cultural or religious value, for entertainment and also for the health for healing and as exercise.

HISTORY

Archaeology delivers traces of dance from prehistoric times such as the 30,000-year-old Bhimbetka rock shelters paintings in India and Egyptian tomb paintings depicting dancing figures from 3300BC. Many contemporary dance forms can be traced back to historical, traditional, ceremonial, and ethnic dances of the ancient period.


WHY YOU SHOULD JOIN?

The goal of the association is to show that dancing is fun and can be good for a person's health. The MOVE IT association can also let the youth be more active in physical activities, can serve as an inspiration for anyone, and may further build up your confidence. There is also no specific age limit to join, so that anyone could have the chance to participate!
