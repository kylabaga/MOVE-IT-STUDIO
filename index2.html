MOVE IT

It is the name of the dance studio where dancers can train themselves in dancing a variety type of dances. The association focuses on polishing the members' talent in dancing artistry.

The MOVE IT association is eager to find people who are interested, and have a potential in dancing. It also aims to let the dancers have a better experience individually and as a part of a group.
